Built By Dylan Cathapermal

The Watchlist is a free web application designed to expose new stock traders to a safe and easy way to watch and understand how the market moves, without the risk of trading on a real broker.

Risk-Free

Many people who want to get into trading but are uncertian about linking their financial information to a broker can learn about the market for free through Watchlist
